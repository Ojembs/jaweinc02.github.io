Dispute Resolution Practice Group (DRPG)

We are renowned for the strength of our dispute resolution practice in Nigeria, particularly in the area of litigation and arbitration. In the course of the last thirty years our lawyers ha represented clients in several landmark cases at the Tribunals, High Courts, Court of Appeal and Supreme Court, cutting across virtually all Territory, Abuja. Lawyers in our DRPG routinely represent clients in matters of high value and great complexity at the trial and appellate courts and at all times aim to ensure that

any disputes arising from our Clients’ businesses and transactions are effectively resolved in the quickest and most cost-effective manner.
