Regulatory Compliance and Business Advisory Practice Group (RCBAG)

Our RCBAG works with and advises local and international clients on several matters including the formation of businesses, companies, partnerships and joint ventures, immigration law, property conveyancing, foreign investment and employment law and policies. Over the course of the last twenty-five years lawyers in ourRCBAG have developed and maintained useful relationships with key regulatory

agencies, which provides a significant advantage to our clients with respect to registration and compliance matters and helps in the avoidance of unnecessary costs. Our work in this area typically involves advising on registrations and compliance matters with government agencies such as the Nigerian Investment Promotion Commission, Corporate Affairs Commission, National Agency for Food

and Drug Administration and Control, Nigerian Immigration Service, Federal Inland Revenue Service, Standards Organisation of Nigeria, Lands Registries and Trademarks, Patents and Designs Registry, among others. The RCBAG also provides company secretarial services through TOCSEC Services, which is the Company Secretarial arm of Unknown.
